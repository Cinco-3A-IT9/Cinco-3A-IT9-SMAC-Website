Science Investigatory Project

Introduction and Content

Our SIP is about how different ratios of like 25%, 50%, 75%, and 100% of organic compost can affect the growth of a tomato plant. This study was created by our group in hopes of helping our partner institution, SMAC. This project would help them in both finding a nice therapeutic exercise and an alternate food source.

In this webpage my group will be presenting our:

  • Problem statement
  • Hypothesis
  • Variables
  • Set-Up
  • Materials

Problem Statement



What ratio of organic compost to soil will help the tomato (Solanum lycoperscium) grow better?



Hypothesis



If we used 75% of organic compost to 25% soil then the tomato (Solanum lycoperscium) will grow better



Variables

Independant Variable

Organic Compost(Eggshells, Fruit peels, leaves, sticks)

Dependant Variable

Growth of tomato(Solanum lycoperscium) after the experiment.

Controlled Variables

Amount of water, Amount of sunlight, Type of soil
