Project: Conservation Education

The Missionary Sisters of the Love
of Christ are the organizers of SMAC.
Their aim is to focus on abandoned,
neglected orphans and give them
immediate medical and health care services
that they need. They want to upgrade the quality
of their services for the children and
to produce socially adjusted
and psychologically developed children.

Beneficiaries

The beneficiaries are orphans, they are children that got abandoned,
neglected and are living in very poor living conditions.
Resulting in malnutrition, lacking in hygiene and low morale.
They are also exposed to violence and sexual abuses.

Benefactors

The benefactors of this institution are AdDU Junior High and Irish students from Ireland.
The students go there to do the school activities that the school administers.
There are also others that donate food and money to help the SMAC institution.

The Vission And Mission Of SMAC Institution

Vission

The vision of the institution is to produce:
Socially adjusted and psychologically developed children,
spiritually mature, living and practising Christian values,
sharing their faith with their peers, and enjoying the fullness of life.

Mission

The mission of SMAC is to: Seek out the neglected, abandoned, and orphaned children in the streets, allow them to improve their lives through education; restore their dignity as human beings by giving them a home where they can feel love and care, receive spiritual and values formation to nurture and strengthen their integral human development.

How The Institution Was Created

The institution officially opened on the Feast day of Saint Joseph, the foster-father of Jesus. Witnessing the living condition of the poor parishioners and families who are unable to provide treatment for sick members; the presence of out-of-school children due to economic incapacity resulting for most of them to wander on the streets; very poor living condition resulting to malnutrition, lacking in hygiene and low morale; extreme poverty and factors that contribute for the children to be exposed to violence and sexual abuses. These observations have led the SMAC to gather those children and provided them with a home. The SMAC institution is a non-profit organization focusing on abandoned, neglected, and orphans needing immediate medical and health care services.
